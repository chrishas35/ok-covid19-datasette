Statement on Releasing Patient Location Information

March 24, 2020

In order to comply with State and Federal Privacy requirements of individuals affected by COVID-19 and other illnesses, the Oklahoma State Department of Health refrains from releasing the town/city of residence.The OSDH regularly updates the Oklahoma-specific COVID-19 website found at www.coronavirus.health.ok.gov
