OSDH Provides Update on Alert System

September 11, 2020

OKLAHOMA CITY (Sept. 11, 2020) – The color-coding on the Oklahoma COVID-19 Alert System map has always aligned with the color thresholds used by the White House Coronavirus Task Force. It came to our attention today that the legend on the map has incorrectly listed the threshold between the orange and yellow risk phases as 14.39 instead of 14.29.

The data run and the county colors on the map have always been accurate. This was simply a typo in the legend and it has been corrected. Being open and transparent with our data is deeply important to our agency and will continue to be.
